A bit about us

WHO WE ARE

Ciiku Beauty and Wellness Hub is Africa’s leading online shopping destination for skincare, hair care, makeup, wellness and beauty accessories. With over 165 brands and 10,000-plus beauty care products, there's no better place to shop for your favorite beauty brands. Established to focus on providing essential and professional beauty products to you based on quality, convenience and at a bargain from one portal. Ciiku is dedicated to our customer’s beauty, personal care and wellness needs. We provide an extensive selection of proprietary merchandise from both local and international markets.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide quality beauty and wellness products affordably and conveniently. We strive to provide a complete range of cosmetics, creating an enjoyable and fulfilling experience.

OUR VISION

To become the leading beauty and wellness product supplier in Africa and cater to every individual need in these categories.

OUR VALUES

Just as we admire the genuine worth in every woman, we believe that the every woman deserves nothing but the best, purest, authentic beauty and wellness products money can buy. Unlike other online players, we NEVER source anything from the ‘grey market’, choosing to only offer products that are 100% authentic, sourced directly from the brands or authorized distributors. Our authenticity also extends to all aspects of how we run our business. From its very inception, Ciiku has strived to set the benchmark in product quality control so that customers are assured of receiving 100% genuine and fresh products sourced directly from manufacturers or their authorized distributors.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

At Ciiku we believe that beauty comes from within and radiates outwards. Our vision is to help boost women’s confidence and self-esteem and to make them feel beautiful!

WHY US

At Ciiku we have identified that our customers increasingly have more hectic lifestyles and have the need to find some time for themselves. In order to satisfy their needs, we have decided to provide a unique online store that gives a one stop shop solution to all things beauty and wellness. Ciiku hub is an important extension to the image and personality of beauty in terms of pricing, variety of products and ease of access. Consumers will enjoy shopping for their personal brand while at home and enjoy a different kind of experience. This enhanced experience is aligned with our philosophy and vision, making women feel beautiful every moment, everywhere. Ciiku.co.ke will be the core digital platform integrated with all channels: social media presence, blogs, and some retail stores, creating a consistent and a unique brand experience.
